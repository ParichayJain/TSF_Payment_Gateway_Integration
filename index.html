About Us

Umeed - A drop of hope (NGO) is formed to provide a platform to nation youth. Founder: Mr. Shubhrajeet Gautam (January 26, 2011) Currently we are having an outreach of 30,000+ volunteers in 7 states across the country. We are working every single day for the under-privileged sections of our society. Tracing back to the inception, Umeed was established with a small cloth donation campaign as its first stepping milestone for the organisation. The main motive behind the establishment was child welfare.
